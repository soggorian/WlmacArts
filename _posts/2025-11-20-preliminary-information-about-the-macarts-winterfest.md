## Dec 11 | 5:30 - 8:30 pm

* * 5.30 Dance/Drama Show
  * 6:15 Festival -- Food, Carnival Games, Art & Craft Sale (gifts!!), Silent Auction
  * 7:15 Sounds of the Season Music Show
* Audience/Attendees are Mackenzie students and parents

\*\*As a note, for students wishing to buy tickets to attend - there are 3 ticket options:

1. Dance/Drama Show + Festival ($10) -- 5:30 - 7:15
2. Festival + Sounds of the Season Show ($10) -- 6:15 - 8:30
3. Both Shows + Festival ($15) -- 5:30 - 8:30

Tickets can be bought online on School Cash Online when it opens.
